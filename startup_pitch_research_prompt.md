## Research Methodology Instructions

### For Each Section:

1. **Use Multiple Sources**: Gather data from:
   - Industry reports (Gartner, Forrester, McKinsey, etc.)
   - Market research databases (Statista, IBISWorld, etc.)
   - Academic journals and papers
   - Government databases and statistics
   - Competitor websites and materials
   - News articles and press releases
   - Industry expert interviews or publications

2. **Cite All Sources**: Provide proper citations for all data, statistics, and claims

3. **Be Specific**: Use concrete numbers, dates, and examples rather than generalizations

4. **Show Your Work**: Explain assumptions and methodology for projections and estimates

5. **Stay Current**: Prioritize recent data (last 2-3 years) unless historical context is needed

6. **Be Realistic**: Balance optimism with realistic, defensible projections

7. **Identify Gaps**: If critical information is unavailable, clearly note this and explain how you'd obtain it
